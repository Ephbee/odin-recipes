Cheesy Quesadilla

This cheese quesadilla is so simple but so delicious! In less than 10 minutes you can have a cheesy, crispy, warm quesadilla at the ready for a quick lunch or a family-favorite dinner. And the best part? You can add other ingredients to make this a clean-out-the-fridge meal any night of the week!

Quesadilla ingredients:

  • 4 flour tortillas (8 inches), warmed
  • 1-1/2 cups of shredded Mexican cheese blend - don't forget to include cheddar cheese.
  • 1/2 cup of salsa

How to asseble the quesadillas:

  1. Place the tortillas on a greased baking sheet. Combine the cheese and salsa; spread over half of each tortilla. Fold over.
  2. Broil 4 in. from the heat for 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Cut into wedges.
